About Us

Tinsel Titans is a student-run company at MCDigital that specializes in designing and selling high-tech snowball turrets. Born out of a passion for combining holiday spirit with cutting-edge technology, Tinsel Titans delivers a fun, innovative solution for anyone looking to add some festive flair (and defense!) to their winter season.
